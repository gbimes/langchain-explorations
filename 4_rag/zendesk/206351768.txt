<h1>Can I transfer Credits from my Teacher Wallet to my Student Wallet?</h1>
<h3>Yes!</h3>
<p>Many italki Teachers use their italki Credits to learn a language.</p>
<p>To do this, you need to move your italki Credits from your Teacher Wallet to your Student Wallet.</p>
<ol>
<li>Log in as a teacher</li>
<li>Go to "<span class="wysiwyg-underline"><a href="https://teach.italki.com/finance/overview" target="_blank" rel="noopener">My Wallet</a></span>" </li>
<li>Click "<strong>Transfer Credits</strong>"<img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005888326" alt="mceclip0.png">
</li>
<li>Enter the amount of italki Credits you'd like to transfer and your italki password to confirm.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Please note: You cannot transfer the Credits back from your Student Wallet to your Teacher Wallet. </strong></p>
