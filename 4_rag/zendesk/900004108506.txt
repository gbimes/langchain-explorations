<p><strong>---</strong></p>
<p><strong>Voice messages are supported! </strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">On the italki </span><strong>app</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, you can send, receive and play voice messages. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">On the italki </span><strong>web</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, only receiving and playing voice messages are supported.</span></p>
<br>
<p><strong>Files are saved for 30 days </strong></p>
<br>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">On the italki web, only teachers and paying users can send </span><strong>images and files.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">On the italki app, all users can send </span><strong>images</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, Only teachers and paying users can send files.</span></p>
<br>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Files will be saved on italki for 30 days. Make sure you download the file you need and save it locally before it expires! </span></p>
