<h2><strong>How does the payment works</strong></h2>
<p>User needs to pay $5 dollar first and book lessons have a free trial filter, when the free trial is ended, they can choose to get a refund in either italki credit or the original payment method. The refund is automatically processed back to the original payment method. </p>
<p>Please note that after <strong>30 days</strong> of the original payment date, the refund through the website is not possible, so the user will need to email us and follow the normal refund process. </p>
<h2><strong>Cancellation &amp; Reschedule Policy</strong></h2>
<p><strong>* Only reschedule is allowed on Trial lesson, cancellation is not allowed on Trial lesson. We will not cancel any trial lesson in any circumstances </strong></p>
<p><strong>* There will be no "report problem" button. </strong></p>
<p><strong>* For any dispute, please investigate first following the normal process, asking the teacher or student to provide evidence. </strong></p>
<p><strong>* If there is more than one lesson teacher miss, please make sure you copy the first sticky note content and add the 2nd piece of information. For example:</strong></p>
<p>Agent A:  didn't show up trial lesson (Lesson ID 1) </p>
<p>Agent B:  didn't show up trial lesson (Lesson ID 1) </p>
<p><strong>             </strong>  didn't show up trial lesson (Lesson ID 2) </p>
<p><strong>* Please handle the ticket according to the below guideline. Only escalate the ticket when you need to reset the trial. </strong></p>
