<h1>Student Group Class Rules</h1>
<h2 id="Notice" data-renderer-start-pos="2"><strong>What is Group Class？</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10">Group Classes are online classes for 2-6 students that focus on teacher-created topics. You can talk and interact with the teacher and fellow students from around the world to practice your speaking, listening, and other language skills.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10">Please note that for now, Group Classes can only be booked through website: <a href="https://www.italki.com/en/group-class" target="_self">https://www.italki.com/group-class</a>
</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="In-Class-Policy" data-renderer-start-pos="895"><strong>In-Class Policy</strong></h2>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="915">Group Classes are taught via Zoom, and the Zoom meeting ID and password information will appear 10 minutes before the class time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1048">Make sure to attend the class on time, there is no cancellation/reschedule/refund if students are late for the class. However, students are able to enter an ongoing class after the start time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1244">The class length is based on the Class Settings, regardless of whether you interact with the teacher or other students during class time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1385">Inappropriate behaviors are unacceptable and may result in students being warned or removed from class. Inappropriate behaviors include but are not limited to: harassment; sexual/racial/religious discrimination and any other behaviors that negatively influence the group class experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1678">Teachers have the right to remove students from class if they misbehave. Once removed students are not allowed to enter the Group Class again.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1824">Students may turn off their cameras if they choose.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="Credit-Refund-Policy" data-renderer-start-pos="1882"><strong>Credit Refund Policy</strong></h2>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1906">Students can cancel the class request up to 24 hour<span id="c821ca75-be3a-489c-b421-6508849c743b" class="inline-highlight" data-renderer-mark="true" data-mark-type="annotation" data-mark-annotation-type="inlineComment" data-id="c821ca75-be3a-489c-b421-6508849c743b">s before the start of class, and receive a full refund of class credits back to their student wallet. Students are not allowed to cancel the class within 24 hours of the class start time.</span></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2150">Students who apply for full or partial <span id="721feb74-d667-4af7-b336-72d8dbe6f896" data-renderer-mark="true" data-mark-type="annotation" data-mark-annotation-type="inlineComment" data-id="721feb74-d667-4af7-b336-72d8dbe6f896">credit refund</span>s are required to provide evidence, such as screen recordings or chat records to support their request.</p>
