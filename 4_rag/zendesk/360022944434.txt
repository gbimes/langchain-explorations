<h1>How to search for a teacher that fits me most?</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">italki allows users to find a teacher that suits their needs the most. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">So out of 30,000+ teachers, what should you know to select the best one for YOU? </span></p>
<p>Different filters can be used to select the best teacher for you based on your preferences.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005229546/pasted_image_0_10_.png" alt="pasted_image_0_10_.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Move the cursor to the language bar. You can check the teacher's language level.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005229566/pasted_image_010OEVWSS.png" alt="pasted_image_010OEVWSS.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p>If you are looking for specific types of lessons, filter by lesson “<strong>Category</strong>”.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900006153883/pasted_image_0_10_.png" alt="pasted_image_0_10_.png"></p>
<p>Other optional filters are prices, available time slots (displayed in your timezone), lesson categories (business, exam prep, etc.), native speakers, and so on.</p>
<p>Furthermore, you can check the factors below as a reference:</p>
<ul>
<li>Rating scores</li>
<li>Written and video introductions</li>
<li>Lesson description</li>
<li>Background and verified credentials</li>
<li>Lesson statistics and student feedback</li>
</ul>
<p>We suggest that you c<span style="font-weight: 400;">lick the “contact teacher” button to fill in a contact form and tell your teacher more about</span> your study plans, learning goals, preferences, etc.</p>
