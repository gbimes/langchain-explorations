<h1>How do I add my phone number?</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you would like to add your phone number to italki, you can: </span></p>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Go to your account </span><a href="https://www.italki.com/settings/account" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Settings</a> page</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Click “<strong>Add Phone</strong>” </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enter your italki password to verify your identity</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enter your phone number and click “<strong>Send</strong>”</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enter the verification code you have received and click “<strong>Save</strong>”</span></li>
</ol>
