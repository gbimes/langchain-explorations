<h2><strong>FAQ:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>1. When will the participants get the voucher if they achieve 12,16,20,24 hrs?</strong></p>
<p>The 12,16,20,24 hrs reward is the system awards, the system calculates once an hour. For example: if someone arrives at "20h" at 12:05 pm, they will receive the voucher between 13:00 pm-14:00 pm.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>2. When will the top 3-50 winners get the prize?</strong></p>
<p>The top 3-50 prizes need to be counted and then distributed after the campaign ends. The corresponding credits are expected to be distributed on Nov 26th <span>6:00 am (UTC+0)</span>. $50 credit will be topped up to the winner's student wallet directly.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>3. Where can I check the participants' info for LC in OMS?</strong></p>
<p>Please go to the language challenge - Challenge participants and insert user ID for checking.</p>
