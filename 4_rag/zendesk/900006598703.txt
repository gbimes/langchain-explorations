<h2><strong>FAQ:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>1. How is the old referral settled?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In order to make sure we have a smooth transition period, anyone who registered through the previous referral system </span><strong>within the past year</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, but </span><strong>has not made a first purchase before April 14, 2021 </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">is eligible to receive the new referral rewards. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Users who have </span><strong>already received referral rewards</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> from the previous referral program </span><strong>will not be eligible</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>2. Will the inviter and invitee get a coupon automatically after the invitee finished 5/10/25 lessons?</strong></p>
<p>The invitee will need to claim the coupon and the coupon will be deposited to their italki wallet.</p>
<p>Inviter will get the rewards automatically.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>3.What is the "first lesson"?</strong></p>
<p>Both inviter and invitee will get the credit only after the first lesson is "completed".</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>4.Why referral doesn't support email registration？</strong></p>
<p>"防止薅羊毛“。To avoid Fraudulent or cheating behavior on referral, especially for old users who might use their email to get the referral rewards on purpose.</p>
