<h1>Is my language open for application?</h1>
<h3 id="h_01F20V9GPB2SH3MPWAA66EF65W"><strong>Must read</strong></h3>
<p>Please check if all the languages you intend to teach are open for application.<br>e.g. You intend to teach 2 languages - English and Korean. If English is closed and Korean is open this week, you can only teach Korean.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3 id="h_01EV8BHVC07JQH3J2JQYFMAHMC"><strong>List of open languages</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Languages not listed in the table below are all open</li>
<li class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">There is no definite time frame for when a language will reopen</li>
<li class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">The list updates every Monday (UTC +8), <strong>but languages may close at any time if enough applicants have applied.</strong> </li>
</ul>
