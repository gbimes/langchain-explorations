<h1>How to submit a lesson incomplete request?</h1>
<h2 id="h_01J0QZ7FHRJQ8YFPX7GHEE6E6S"><strong>When and how to submit an incomplete lesson request?</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The student and the teacher have</span><strong> 72 hours</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to submit a lesson incomplete request since a lesson will be automatically confirmed as completed </span><strong>after 72 hours of the lesson time</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and lesson credits will be transferred to the teacher.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If there is an issue with your lesson, make sure you stay in touch with your teacher or turn to italki in time to solve it.</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1. Click "</span><a href="http://www.italki.com/lessons" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>My Lessons</strong></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">" and find the lesson under the "<strong>Action Required</strong></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">" category.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2. Click “Lesson incomplete” on the right side of the lesson page.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdmTxOdX_uVu3xxI3eIwmskPXbSt2HgekAvx8DVncy2LFLtgWD54TNn4UBM7ky38Uy-ifQnRk0HuJw160tvseTdkhlQVRFYEuW_coRKvVzKlhVt_HfCHNu130S5Z16P_NZOQio8FcZhDFhiXJiR6XgK5ufs?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="695" height="399"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3. Select a reason and your expected solution, upload proof and write your explanation.</span></p>
<p><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXddayV1WBgfFnFxn7VBZLrDGW7pr7b9oF3INW7_eeXx4ysbgf_8nB2OHZPljwZqtEbh8rjwzfBlN0-F39lA2RdBNDHkSSjvLXbl-d8PL9EwEnLNw8nOBhTemBFWu66zyBqxgkizyBTRQ_vFjitWrmL2vkh7?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="231" height="286"><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeU_qTeE_DAeikzzDdcK91NAQ520Rnzbrr1NYIx55E8yGk5Ab_ZyfnNKqRlraMi6LHIt4WWFQqdjkYd3SG0PIkeLBjX9b1yugbFnrkeYo2vMsYMZeGPlpEwBGTXfkAxQgItzOyK7dG02-ktDPlI-B8wOW_M?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="233" height="288"><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd2GBkeXkBcRi2zIAYBWd1uXrgX74hfEjf7HsaLyqNov-5czWrwHawku-BFB6h6rBfBAmfoz53h6XbHRAMWu7JAK0_Y-WSltjANXV97kHQtjHz99t5rVEOgLhwbG41XzBmBpAeo2YHmGke_VL5WUi-0MoTu?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="234" height="290"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4. Click "<strong>Submit</strong></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">" and wait for the reply.</span></p>
<p><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeTP2_z1NxRJwMcHaP0G2ZBIxFrTGBt4Gkr_qadpcKDmY6owep2YUrOf-82_a-7UP5XSXmYJIz2DQXVrX3xjJROstEQ4Qs5aJYjOG_8EFgl_tvc8xMLiGZ73nLygLrh7gP6WLco8gC35x_qB0HQvuKoIa10?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="696" height="323"></p>
<p>The other party has <strong>3 days</strong> to respond to the request. <strong>If the request is accepted,</strong> it will be resolved in accordance with the solution proposed.</p>
<p><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXds0agkPB1epEBzJdSw0mpgOf2Gr7j--a27icSfgneesm4LkamAWgpUbXbpyW2mVULuHNwPW8KB7wK2XP9HOKqVpdbfpksd0o8DD-QXPEozpeVRHsH_eHStAW3msgKNdFbUfnvRO-jxUK1MyVQQ3GbPOHgz?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="694" height="293"></p>
<p><strong>If the request is declined,</strong> you will see the reason and solution submitted by the teacher. Then you get to choose either to accept or decline the request.</p>
<p><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcjKgP45b1ktXSSL8H6v0STtYtqXssIGHSOAhXGhl7yFPV5QgFbsnbnBDSvTtkIeMSNcCfXhbXZaOm9XvLoQ8gx2qowTuk2E5GQIB6qSvC85wlDqr0EkvV-M3VKZZEjvX1tDqnTfUQtJqNo5DvD16nJKLtR?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="696" height="321"></p>
<p><strong>If you accept the request</strong>, the lesson issue will be resolved.</p>
<p><strong>If you decline the request or take no action in 3 days</strong>, it will become a dispute and italki will make the final decision based on the evidence submitted by both parties.</p>

<p> </p>
<h2 id="h_01HFV7513GBJ4HHPZZQVF4ZH21"><strong>Can I withdraw the lesson incomplete request?</strong></h2>
<p><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>As long as the other party hasn’t responded to your request</strong>, you can click on “withdraw request” to cancel it. You have up to <strong>3 days</strong> to withdraw your request (starting from the reporting day).</span></strong></p>
<p><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeHkRiMmmisDAd2sLwgICQE7-gNlb_D5i_3qh_kZdlN9fCNANlTMBqPZnRsshuQNIVVLEuKRQ_ceNsi359cw5jArFlX9OOja8l-gE4EWxPuA4Ct3cDSkzk4Q-bCnkxRniOUuCOIzGNKzf5PZyARdAYJGima?key=xFRnkVXgDChnllT3eojS1g" width="696" height="327"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you withdraw the request <strong>within 72 hours after lesson time</strong>, you can:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">Submit a new lesson incomplete request</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Complete the lesson and leave lesson feedback normally </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"></span>
</li>
</ul>
<p>If you withdraw the lesson incomplete request <strong>72 hours after lesson time</strong>, the lesson will be <strong>marked as completed</strong> with lesson credits transferred to the teacher.</p>
