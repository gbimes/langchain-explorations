<h1>How to create Jira form </h1>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large">When user reports an issue or provides product feedback, we can directly create Jira ticket through zendesk and our product team will review it on a regular basis to improve our user experience. </span></p>

<h2><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large">2.3 Summary* </span></h2>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large">Summarize the problem </span></p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large wysiwyg-color-green130">Good example: italki classroom issue - Lesson ID xxxxxxxxxx</span></p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large wysiwyg-color-red70">Bad example: User can't book lesson</span><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><span></span></span></p>

<h2>2.5 优先级（Priority）</h2>
<p>Please select priority based on your understanding of the issue, in general, anything related to can't pay, can't book a lesson or can't register as a user is a high priority. </p>
