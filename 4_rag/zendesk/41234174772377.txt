<h1>How to redeem a Clozemaster code</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JG3G14AE5FBW0MKFXEKS6GN5">About Clozemaster</h2>
<p>Clozemaster is the fastest way to expand your vocabulary in another language. Learn vocabulary in context, improve your listening skills, and level up your grammar by playing through thousands of sentences. Get fluent faster. Start today!</p>
<h2 id="h_01JG3G14AEBDKY6RVW7TSKD1XT">Redeem your code</h2>
<p>1. Sign up <a href="https://www.clozemaster.com/sign-up" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a> or log in.</p>
<p>2. Navigate to <a href="https://www.clozemaster.com/pro" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Clozemaster Pro</a>.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41675163671449" width="699" height="278"></p>
<p>3. Select the <strong>Monthly</strong> plan below. Please note that the discount codes can only be applied to the monthly option.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41675168531609" width="2048" height="904"></p>
<p>4. Tap the “Add Discount” button to enter your discount code then continue to the next step.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41675168535833" width="1600" height="936"></p>
<p>You'll need to enter your card details to start the promotion. But don't worry, you won't be charged for the first month, and you can cancel the subscription anytime before the next payment date.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41675168537497" width="1534" height="910"></p>
<p>If you have issues with redeeming your code, please contact support at <a href="mailto:mike@clozemaster.com"><span class="wysiwyg-underline" style="color: #1155cc;">mike@clozemaster.com</span></a>.</p>
