<h1>Hyperwallet transaction fees and countries covered</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large" style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Local Bank Transfer</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hyperwallet transfers the money to your local bank account resulting in lower transaction fees compared to Wire Transfer. If a currency exchange is required, Hyperwallet will carry out the conversion at a rate set by Hyperwallet.</span></p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Wire Transfer (SWIFT)</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Wire transfer is a traditional method of sending money internationally via banks which use the SWIFT system. In some cases, both the sending and receiving banks may charge fees resulting in higher transaction costs.</span></p>
