<h2><strong>FAQ</strong></h2>
<p><strong>1. Who will get “Buy$10 get $9 off” coupon? when will the coupon get expired?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">New CN users who registered through the campaign landing page during the campaign period. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“Buy$10 get $9 off” coupon will expire after the campaign period.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>2.How to check if the user is registered through the campaign landing page in OMS?</strong></p>
<p>Users who successfully register through the <span style="font-weight: 400;">campaign </span>landing page should be able to receive the“Buy$10 get $9 off” coupon. You can go to vouchers — voucher list to check. If nothing is found, that means the user didn't register from the landing page.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/4402006096537/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>3. How “Buy$10 get $9 off” coupon works?</strong></p>
<p>Basically, you can consider the “Buy$10 get $9 off” coupon as a top-up discount. Users can get $10 by paying $1.<span style="font-weight: 400;"></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>4. How to get “Buy $60 get $5 free” coupon?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">New CN users who registered during the campaign period, successfully completed the first lesson. When they confirm the lesson completed, the system will automatically issue the “Buy $60 get $5 free” coupon to their student wallet. The coupon will valid for 30 days from the time user receive it. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>5. How to define "existing users"?</strong></p>
<p>Existing users mean Mainland Chinese users who are active (at least login to the website) in the past 12 months. Mkt team will issue the <span style="font-weight: 400;">“Buy $500 get $50 free” &amp; “Buy $800 get $88 free” to these users' accounts directly. <span class="wysiwyg-color-red">(For users who registered from June 9th, the Mkt team will manually offer the coupon to their account one day after the registration date)</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span></p>
<p><strong>6. How come that I topped up $800 from the App but only get $50 extra in the end?</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"></span></p>
<p>Users need to select <span style="font-weight: 400;"> “Buy $800 get $88 free” coupon in App during payment. If they didn't select the correct coupon, they might get $50 extra instead.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900008969786/mceclip8.png" alt="mceclip8.png" width="292" height="472"> <img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900009935523/mceclip7.png" alt="mceclip7.png" width="216" height="456"></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>7. What if I forgot to use the “Buy $500 get $50 free” &amp; “Buy $800 get $88 free”?</strong></span></p>
<p>We can ask user to made a new purchase use by ticking the correct coupon. After the payment is completed, we can help to proceed the refund.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>8. During live streaming, I paid 6.18CNY for $10 credit. How can I claim the credits? (18 quotas in total)</strong></p>
<p>User will need to place the order from the shopping list. They need to leave their User ID while purchasing. The $10 credit will be sent to their student account within 3 business days.</p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-color-red">*In case the user left the wrong ID, the payment will be considered invalid. If the user forgot to left user ID, please record in the <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qENJmcKebaPT3mXyf0aevSrIScV-Gf4pvSsXolm6Djw/edit?usp=sharing" target="_self">google sheet</a> and the MKT team will manually offer the credits.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>9.How to claim my rewards for italki T-shirt? (6 quotas in total)</strong></p>
<p>There will a lucky draw session in live streaming. The winner will need to contact <span style="font-weight: 400;">WeChat Channels(italki外教1对1) and leave the shipping adress.</span></p>
