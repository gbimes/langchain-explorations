<p><strong>2) Invalidation of reminder</strong></p>
<p>In principle, the reminder will last for 7 days.<br>Within 7 days, if a scheduled lesson occurs, the reminder will become invalid.</p>
<p><br><strong>3) Re-triggered reminder</strong></p>
<p>In principle, as long as there are completed class hours, the reminder will be triggered indefinitely.</p>
<p><br><strong>4) List sorting</strong></p>
<p>Sort according to the time of the trigger timing, the order is reverse.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-color-red90">- The reminder and/or entry point will be expired after 7 days if the teacher doesn't take any action. </span></p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-color-red90">- The reminder and/or entry point is invisible if the prompt message sent once.</span></p>
<p> </p>
