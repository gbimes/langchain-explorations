<h1>How to cancel a lesson?</h1>
<p><strong>On italki, a lesson can only be canceled or rescheduled 24 hours before the lesson start time.</strong> <span style="font-weight: 400;">If your lesson takes place after 24 hours, you can go to the lesson page and request to "change date/time" of the lesson or "cancel the lesson".</span></p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/25785492378009"></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The lesson will be canceled directly after submitting the cancellation request.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">The lesson credits will be transferred back to the </span><strong><a href="https://www.italki.com/finance/overview" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Student Wallet</a></strong>. For the package lesson, a lesson will be added back to the package.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><strong>If you request "change date/time", the lesson will only be rescheduled when the other party agrees to the request. Otherwise, the lesson will proceed at the originally scheduled time.</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If a lesson is about to start within 24 hours, it can NOT be changed to a new time or canceled. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span>If you can't take a lesson, make sure you notify your teacher at your earliest convenience. Talk with your teacher and see if a refund can be arranged. </span></span><strong>If your refund request is not agreed upon, your teacher is entitled to receive full payment.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">After any lesson, a "Lesson incomplete" button will be available for both teacher and student to propose a lesson refund within 72 hrs.
