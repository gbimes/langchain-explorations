<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Teacher didn’t show up</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Teacher didn’t meet your expectations for the class</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The trial lesson was purchased &amp; paid for by you (*<em>Lessons bought using vouchers are not eligible</em>)</span></li>

<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lesson ID</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The reasons that you are not satisfied</span></li>
