<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">italki works with different Payment Providers to help you withdraw your money in </span><strong>USD</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. The main Payment Providers we use are:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<strong>PayPal</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> - https://www.paypal.com (</span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/206351818-Withdraw-using-PayPal" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Learn More</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">) </span><a href="https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/country-worldwide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">list of countries and currencies supported by Paypal</a>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<strong>Hyperwallet </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">- a PayPal service with a lower service fee (</span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/900001980846-Get-Paid-With-Hyperwallet" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Learn More</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">)</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<strong>Payoneer</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> - https://www.payoneer.com (</span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/360020069633-Withdraw-using-Payoneer" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Learn More</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">)</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<strong>Bank Transfer via Payoneer</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> - italki transfers money through Payoneer to the bank account specified in your Payoneer account. (</span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/206351808-Withdraw-using-Bank-Transfer-via-Payoneer" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Learn More</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">)</span>
</li>
</ul>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-color-red" style="font-weight: 400;">The minimum withdrawal amount is $30 USD in italki Credits.</span></p>
<p> </p>

Please be aware that different countries may have different transaction fees charged by the respective payment provider.</span></p>
