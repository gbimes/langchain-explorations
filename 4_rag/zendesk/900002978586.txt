<h1>What are the technology requirements for teachers?</h1>
<p><strong>Stable internet connection</strong></p>
<p>You can test your connection speed <a href="http://www.speedtest.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a> and improve your connection speed <a href="http://www.wikihow.com/Maximize-the-Speed-of-Your-Internet-Connection" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Microphone and webcam</strong></p>
<p><strong>You must have a microphone and a webcam. Headphones are also recommended.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Using an external camera or microphone typically provides better audio/video quality than using your device's built-in camera or microphone.</span></p>
