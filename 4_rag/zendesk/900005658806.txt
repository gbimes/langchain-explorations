<h1>What is italki Referral Program? </h1>
<h2 id="h_01HX6DCWQY9D0TEB0PQBC1APPF"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/32098698034329"></h2>
<h2 id="h_01HX6DCWQYT9PJ9A96Q92FKXRR"><strong>Refer a Friend</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Refer a Friend is italki’s referral program where you can earn credits and coupons for inviting your friends to italki! Earn $10 for each friend you invite after they purchase $20 italki credits. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you already have an italki account, you can start inviting people from your Referral Dashboard located here: </span><a href="https://www.italki.com/referral" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Link</a></p>
<h2 id="h_01HX6DCWQY9J813Y23FR2K88RD"><strong>When will I get a referral bonus?</strong></h2>
<p><span>When an invitee signs up a <strong>new</strong> italki account <strong>through an inviter’s referral link</strong> and <strong>then successfully</strong> <strong>purchased $20 italki credits or more</strong>, the inviter will get $10 and the invitee will receive $5 italki Credits in the Student Wallet as a referral bonus.</span></p>
<p> </p>
