<h2><strong>FAQ:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>1. What is the definition of a cheerleader?</strong></p>
<p>When the user signs up for the language challenge and completed at least one lesson，after he/she successfully invites friends to sign up, the user is considered a cheerleader.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900008550586/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>2. How much can a cheerleader get for a successful referral?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One cheerleader will receive a $1 cheerleader reward for every friend he/she invites. If cheerleader took at least 1 lesson during the Language Challenge, we’ll sent $1 for each friend that cheerleader invited to their Wallet after the challenge has ended (6:00 AM UTC on 13 July). The maximum number of $1 signup rewards you can receive is 20. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For referral bonus, cheerleaders will automatically receive $15 italki Credits for each friend that completes their first lesson and earn extra rewards as their friends take more lessons, and they can track their progress at https://www.italki.com/referral.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>3. In which form will the winners receive the ranking rewards?</strong></p>
<p>We're going to deposit $50 in credits to the top 50 students and $500 in credits to the top 3 students from <time class="date-future" datetime="2021-07-13">13 Jul 2021.</time></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>4. When will the top 10 winners get the physical medal?</strong></p>
<p>There will be a link on the landing page with a survey where we'll be collecting the user's information. Shipping will starts<span> </span><strong>after 20 July, from China.</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>5. Where can users who achieved 24 hours see the credit $30 for the language test?</strong></p>
<p>The section with the Language Test will have the call-to-action <span class="wysiwyg-color-red">'GO TO WALLET' for the users using mobile'</span> and<span class="wysiwyg-color-red"> 'GO TO THE TEST' for the users using desktop</span>; this is because the Language Test landing page is currently available on desktop only. </p>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900009471843/mceclip3.png" alt="mceclip3.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>6. Can the user spent $30 reward for lesson purchase rather than language test purchase?</strong></p>
<p>Yes. The $30 is basically a credit sent to users. They can use it for lesson purchases. However, if the user asks about the usage, please reply with the link of the language test: <a href="https://www.italki.com/languageassessment/ila">https://www.italki.com/languageassessment/ila</a></p>
<p> </p>
<p>7. Will winners for 24 hours get a notification for the $30 credits?</p>
<p>Yes. Winners for 24 hours will get a message in "Account Notification", please refer to the template below.</p>
