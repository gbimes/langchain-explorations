<h1>Vouchers & Promotions</h1>
<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="1"><strong>Currently, we have three types of promotions</strong></h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="64"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">1.Voucher Codes</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="64">Directly redeem into italki credits, no other action required other than entering it.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="87">Example: FHFIE2E8VIX28B68<img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/4402669169305/mceclip3.png" alt="mceclip3.png"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="230"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">2.Promotion Codes </strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="230">Used when making a purchase, can get a bonus or discount</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="255">Example: XMAS2021<br><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/4402669142681/mceclip1.png" alt="mceclip1.png"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="400"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">3.Coupons</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="400">1-1 coupon sent directly to a user’s wallet. Used when making a purchase, can get a bonus or a discount,</p>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="400">Example: Buy 10 get 5, Buy 10 get 5 off<img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/4402669179929/mceclip5.png" alt="mceclip5.png">
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong><span class="wysiwyg-color-red">* Lesson Coupon refund rules: </span></strong></p>

<p>If the amount refund to student = lesson coupon value, we refund in coupon only</p>
<p>If the amount refund to student &lt; lesson coupon value,<strong> no refund will be given</strong></p>
