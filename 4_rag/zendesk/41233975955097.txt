<h1>How to redeem a LingQ code</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JG3E085DBXX8RN6DAHCXH2YY">About LingQ</h2>
<p>Everyone learns to speak their native language. Why not use the same approach with a second language? Surround yourself with meaningful input that matters to you. You choose what to study. In addition to our huge course library you can import anything into LingQ and instantly turn it into an interactive lesson - books, articles, songs, podcasts even emails...you decide.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JG3E085DH32R0Q2J3BRTGXMW">Redeem your code</h2>
<ol>
<li>Sign up <a href="https://www.lingq.com/en/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a> to create your LingQ account.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent1"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41673667038361" width="692" height="379"></p>
<ol start="2">
<li>Go to Settings.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent1"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41673604090265" width="691" height="326"></p>
<ol start="3">
<li>Follow the instructions below. Once you have successfully redeemed your code, click “Change plan”.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent1"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41673667043609" width="2048" height="803"></p>
<ol start="4">
<li>Select either of the membership levels you want, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent1"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41673604093465" width="689" height="434"></p>
<ol start="5">
<li>You'll need to enter your card details to start the promotion. But don't worry, you won't be charged for the first 2 months, and you can cancel the subscription anytime before the next payment date.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent1"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/41673667072281" width="2048" height="1214"></p>
<p>If you have issues with redeeming your code, please contact support at <a href="mailto:support@lingq.com"><span class="wysiwyg-underline" style="color: #1155cc;">support@lingq.com</span></a>.</p>
