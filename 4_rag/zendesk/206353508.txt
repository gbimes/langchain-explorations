<h1>Privacy settings on italki</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hover on your Profile Picture and click </span><strong>Settings </strong>to<span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">visit your italki </span></strong><a href="https://www.italki.com/settings/account" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Settings</a> page.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900003600866" alt="1.4.1-Hoever-Settings.png"></span></p>
<p id="h_01EQ08AMTHXVDMSR4SHKKDG8AM"><span>Select the </span><strong>Privacy </strong><span>tab. </span><span>You can</span></p>
<ul>
<li id="h_01EQ089T0HDHHYX23PEC11DYGK"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Update your <strong>Contact Permissions</strong> and decide who can send you messages.</span></li>
<li><span>Edit your <strong>block list</strong></span></li>
<li>
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Disable </span><strong>Other Privacy settings</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> if you do not want your following, lesson feedback, or age to be public. </span>
</li>
</ul>
