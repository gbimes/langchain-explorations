<h1>Instant Lesson</h1>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="120"><span>Instant Lesson allows students to book trial lessons right away. These lessons are automatically accepted and can’t be declined. Instant Lessons start 5 minutes after a student purchases.</span></p>
<ol class="ak-ol" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="329">Instant Lessons are currently only available for 30 minutes <strong>trial lessons</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="407">These lessons are automatically accepted and start 5 minutes after purchase.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="487">If students have an upcoming lesson within the next 40 minutes, student<span id="342e15a9-09c1-423a-bcd2-efdf41f07a95" class="inline-highlight" data-renderer-mark="true" data-mark-type="annotation" data-mark-annotation-type="inlineComment" data-id="342e15a9-09c1-423a-bcd2-efdf41f07a95"> can't book an Instant Lesson.</span></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="583">All Instant Lessons must be taken in italki Classroom.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="641">Instant lessons cannot be canceled or rescheduled.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="754"><span class="wysiwyg-color-cyan110"><strong>Teacher Side</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Under the teacher dashboard, the teacher will be asked to enable instant lessons. After read through the definition of instant lesson and lesson policy, the teacher will be required to sign up to enable this function.</li>
<li>If the teacher not yet setting up any trial lesson, the system will ask the teacher to complete the trial lesson setting then.</li>
<li>Teachers needs to turn on system notifications to enable instant lesson as well.</li>
</ul>

<p data-renderer-start-pos="1640"><strong>Instant Lesson will be disabled in the following situations:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1704">Teachers are inactive for 6 hours (<span class="wysiwyg-color-red80">inactive means no switching pages or clicking on functions on the web or APP )</span></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1736">After completing an Instant Lesson or having no available time slots for the next 40 minutes.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1836"><strong>Instant Lessons follow all standard italki Lesson policies as well as the following:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2046">Instant Lessons <span id="a9258ab8-2129-4dbb-bc8c-f733f87bc13b" class="inline-highlight" data-renderer-mark="true" data-mark-type="annotation" data-mark-annotation-type="inlineComment" data-id="a9258ab8-2129-4dbb-bc8c-f733f87bc13b">override teacher's</span> auto-accept settings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2099">Instant Lessons cannot be canceled or rescheduled.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Teachers are unable to change the following functions after enabling instant lessons:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1044">Vacation mode</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1061">Lesson request settings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1088">Trial lesson settings</p>
</li>
</ul>
