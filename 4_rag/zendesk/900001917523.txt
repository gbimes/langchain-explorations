<h1>Hyperwallet Payee Verification </h1>
<h2><strong>What’s Hyperwallet identity verification and why do I need to do it?</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hyperwallet is a</span><strong> PayPal service</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> that provides fast, efficient, and transparent payment services throughout the globe. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In order to ensure compliance with AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and CTF (Counter Terrorist Financing) regulations, Hyperwallet requires some teachers to</span><strong> verify their identity</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This process only applies to teachers located in the EEA (European Economic Area). This includes teachers who are European citizens and teachers who currently reside in the EU.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Teachers will be asked to undergo identity verification when their </span><strong>accumulated </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">withdrawal amount reaches 2,500 EUR (3,000 USD) within 365 days from their first successful withdrawal.   </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For example, if you’re a teacher in Europe who recently withdrew 1,000 EUR through the updated PayPal, you would not be asked to verify your identity until your withdrawals totaled 2,500 EUR.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Verifications can take 1 - 3 business days to be processed and will be</span><strong> reviewed by the PayPal Compliance team</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.  </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you are required to undergo identity verification, all transfers out of your account will be </span><strong>paused </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">until the verification is complete.</span></p>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><strong>What documents do I need for the verification process? </strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The following documents may be requested to complete the verification:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Proof of identification, such as one of the following:</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Passport</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Driver's license</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Government ID card</span></li>
</ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Proof of address, such as one of the following:</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bank or credit card statement</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Utility bill</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Government letter</span></li>
</ul>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Document requirements</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Both front and back of the document must be submitted, except for passports.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Name on the document must be an exact match with the name of the payee.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Documents must be dated and valid (expired documents are not permitted).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">All four corners must be clearly visible.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Documents must be in color.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Document must be readable and of good quality.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Size: min 400KB – max 4MB</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Format: .pdf, .jpg or .png</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Proof of address documents will be validated based on the following requirements:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Name on the document must be an exact match with the name of the payee.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Address on the document must be an exact match with the address of the payee.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Document must be dated and should not be more than 90 days old.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Document must be readable and of good quality.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Size: min 30KB – max 4MB</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Format: .pdf, .jpg or .png</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If your documents don’t meet these requirements, the verification won’t be successful.</span></p>
