<h1>Difference between Professional Teacher and Community Tutor</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">There are two types of teaching profiles on italki - </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Professional Teacher and Community Tutor.</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> E</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">ach applicant can only choose </span><strong>one</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> teacher type. You cannot apply to be an English Professional Teacher and Italian Community Tutor for example. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Professional Teacher</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Professional teachers have training as educators and/or extensive professional teaching experience. They can provide students with resources, prepared materials, and structured lesson plans to help students reach their goals.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">To apply for a professional teaching profile, you must upload </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">documents</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> showing your training and experience as an educator. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Check the minimum requirements </span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/206352058-What-types-of-certification-or-credentials-are-required-for-a-Professional-Teaching-Profile-" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Community Tutor</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Community tutors are friendly, helpful, knowledgeable, and use their time to provide students with conversation practice. </span></li>
</ul>
