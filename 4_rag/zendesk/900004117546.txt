<h1>How do I find my personal Zoom link, ID and Passcode?</h1>
<h3>How do I find my Zoom link, ID and passcode?</h3>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sign in to the Zoom website.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Click Meetings, then Personal Room.</span></li>
</ol>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/36792936602009" alt="Screenshot_2021-01-26_at_16.53.38.png"></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">On the Zoom app interface, select the “Meeting” menu to find your meeting ID, meeting link and passcode.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/36792936604441" alt="Screenshot_2021-01-26_at_16.54.56.png"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For more information, please refer to this </span><a href="https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362843-Personal-meeting-ID-PMI-and-personal-link" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Zoom Help Guide</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<h3>How do I add Zoom as one of my Communication Tools?</h3>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Go to </span><a href="https://teach.italki.com/console/basic" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://teach.italki.com/console/basic</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, then click “+Add a communication tool”.</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fill in your personal meeting link, personal meeting ID, and passcode.</span></li>
</ol>

<h3>How do your students attend a lesson via Zoom?</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your student may click the <strong>“Enter Classroom via Zoom”</strong> button on the lesson detail page or log into Zoom directly with the Zoom meeting ID and passcode provided on the lesson detail page.</span></p>
