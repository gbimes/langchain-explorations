<h1>Withdraw using PayPal</h1>
<p dir="ltr">In order to get paid via PayPal, you must have a valid PayPal account that can receive funds.</p>
<p dir="ltr">If you do not have a PayPal account, you must first create one and make sure that your account can receive funds before you can proceed with processing italki Credits. Each country has different requirements for payment via PayPal. Many require that you have to submit your real identification with your country's PayPal website. </p>
<p dir="ltr">Please contact your country's PayPal website to ensure that your PayPal account can receive funds. If you need to contact PayPal about your account, visit <a href="https://www.paypal.com/selfhelp" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PayPal self help</a>.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Here, you can view <a href="https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/country-worldwide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PayPal's list of supported countries and currencies</a>.</p>
<p dir="ltr"> </p>
<h3 dir="ltr">PayPal Withdrawal Examples</h3>
<p dir="ltr">One of these methods will be automatically chosen to process your withdrawal request. Please note the minimum withdrawal amount is<strong> $30</strong> <strong>USD</strong> in italki Credits. </p>
<p dir="ltr">For a normal or an express withdrawal request, the method will be <strong>PayPal MassPay</strong>. But if it's an exclusive withdrawal request, <strong>PayPal Individual pay</strong> will be applied. </p>

<h3 dir="ltr">Important Information</h3>
<ul>
<li dir="ltr">If MassPay fails due to a problem with the teacher's PayPal account, then italki will use <strong>PayPal Individual Pay</strong> to process the payment. </li>
</ul>
<h4 dir="ltr"> </h4>
<h3 dir="ltr">How to withdraw your credits via PayPal</h3>
<p dir="ltr">1. Add PayPal as your payment method in <a href="https://teach.italki.com/console/withdraw" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Withdrawal Settings</a>.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900008096126" alt="Screenshot_2021-04-28_at_15.20.23.png" width="664" height="262"></p>
<p dir="ltr"> </p>
<p dir="ltr">2. Enter your <strong>PayPal email address</strong> and <strong>italki password</strong>. Your PayPal information will be saved for future withdrawal.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900008096206" alt="Screenshot_2021-04-28_at_15.24.46.png" width="594" height="308"></p>
