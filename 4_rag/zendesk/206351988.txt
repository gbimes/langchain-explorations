<h4><strong>Detailed guidelines and requirements</strong></h4>
<p>Once uploaded, your profile picture will be <strong>circular</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"> <img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/36783607265177" alt="" width="202" height="201"></p>
<ul>
<li>Format: JPG, GIF, or PNG</li>
<li>Maximum size: 2MB; Ideal size: at least 200 x 200 pixels</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use blurry or unclear pictures</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use pictures with a busy background that detracts from you</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use pictures that contain other people</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> wear sunglasses</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use pictures where you are too close to or far away from the camera</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use pictures that are too bright or dark</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not</strong> use pictures that have commercial logos or personal contact information</li>
<li>
<strong>Do not </strong>edit pictures in a way that is misleading or obstructs your face</li>
</ul>
<h4> </h4>
<p><strong>It will take upto 5 business days to review if you update the photo.</strong></p>
<p><strong>If you need to take some time off or are unavailable for teaching</strong>, do not demonstrate this by removing or changing your profile picture to something that does not meet profile requirements. Please manage this in your <strong><a href="https://teach.italki.com/console/lessons" target="_blank" rel="noopener">availability settings</a></strong>.</p>
