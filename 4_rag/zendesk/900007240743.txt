<h1>What’s Ask a Teacher?</h1>
<h3>Ask A Teacher is a new Community feature designed to help learners get corrections and answers from teachers.</h3>
<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="384"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">For Learners:</strong></h2>
<ol class="ak-ol" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="419">Learners will be given the option to ask a teacher when they finish publishing an exercise or a question.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="528">Each learner who just published their post will be shown a list of 5 teachers who teach their learning language. Learners can invite all 5 teachers to help them with their exercise or question.</p>

<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="1033"> </h2>
<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="1033"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Important Information:</strong></h2>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1058">There are a lot of teachers on italki! It’s very possible that you won’t get an invitation immediately after Ask a Teacher is released.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1197">Teachers who help others in the Community usually have <strong data-renderer-mark="true">higher </strong>lesson request rates. Even if you’re not invited to help through Ask a Teacher, you’re still very welcome to answer questions and correct exercises in the italki Community!</p>
</li>
</ul>
