<h1>How do I change my italki login email?</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1. To change the email you use to login to italki, click "Change email" </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005322723/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"><br>2. Enter your italki password to verify your identity.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005322783/mceclip1.png" alt="mceclip1.png"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3. Enter a new email address you’d like to use. Click "save"</span></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://support.italki.com/hc/article_attachments/900005322843/mceclip2.png" alt="mceclip2.png"></strong></p>
<p>4. Go to your email inbox and click the link provided in the email to verify your new email address. </p>
