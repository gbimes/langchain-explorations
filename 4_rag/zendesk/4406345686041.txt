<h1>How to use instant lesson to have a lesson now? </h1>
<h2 data-renderer-start-pos="93"><strong>What are Instant Lessons?</strong></h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="120">Instant Lessons allow you to start a lesson with a teacher right away! After purchasing an Instant Lesson, you’ll be able to start learning within 5 minutes.</p>
<p><strong>Things you should know about Instant Lessons:</strong></p>
<ol class="ak-ol" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="329">Instant Lessons are <strong>available for 30/45/60 minutes trial lessons</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="407">These lessons are automatically accepted and start 5 minutes after purchase.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="487">If you have an upcoming lesson within the next 40/55/70 minutes, <span id="342e15a9-09c1-423a-bcd2-efdf41f07a95" class="inline-highlight" data-renderer-mark="true" data-mark-type="annotation" data-mark-annotation-type="inlineComment" data-id="342e15a9-09c1-423a-bcd2-efdf41f07a95">you can't book an Instant Lesson.</span></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="583">All Instant Lessons must be taken in italki Classroom.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="641">You must arrive at your Instant lesson on time and your Instant lessons cannot be canceled or rescheduled.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="752"> </p>
