<h1>Withdraw using Hyperwallet</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re improving the way you get paid with the launch of our new withdrawal partner - Hyperwallet.</span></p>
<h2><strong>What’s Hyperwallet?</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hyperwallet is a PayPal service that provides fast, efficient, and transparent payment services throughout the globe. </span></p>
<h2><strong>What's changing?</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">From <strong>4 January 2021</strong>, teacher withdrawals deposited into Paypal accounts will be processed directly via Hyperwallet with a </span><strong>lower processing fee of 1.5% </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">compared to Paypal’s 2% fee (</span><a href="https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/206351818-How-do-I-withdraw-my-ITC-using-Paypal-"><span style="font-weight: 400;">How do I withdraw my Credits using PayPal?</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This update will be </span><strong>gradually </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">released to all teachers.</span></p>
<h2><strong>What do I need to do?</strong></h2>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Go to the </span><strong>Teacher Settings </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">page and click </span><strong>Withdrawal (</strong><a href="https://teach.italki.com/console/withdraw"><strong>https://teach.italki.com/console/withdraw</strong></a><strong>) </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">to manage your </span><strong>Paypal </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">settings</span><strong>.</strong>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Connecting/Updating </span><strong>Paypal </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">options<br></span>Click <strong>Update/Connect</strong><span> and enter your personal information to verify your information.</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Confirm/complete your personal information</span> to create the profile.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<span style="font-weight: 400;">Confirm/complete your Paypal account information:<br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">- Your Paypal account details<br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">- Your country (as shown on PayPal)<br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">- Your italki password </span>
</li>
</ol>
