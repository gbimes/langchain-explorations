<h3>Teacher Zendesk Community Post</h3>
<p>Official italki Community Posts:</p>
<ul>
<li>We have an update, announcement, request, invitation, or other official communication for the teacher community from italki. This must be approved by the Community Manager and should be posted by a Zendesk agent account.</li>
</ul>
<p>Replying to posts:</p>
<ul>
<li>Anyone can reply to posts from their own individual account. It should either be an account that meets teacher profile standards or very obviously an italki moderator account.</li>
</ul>
<p>Posting unofficially:</p>
<ul>
<li>Anyone can make a post if they are speaking as themselves and not as a representative of italki. It should be an account that meets teacher profile standards.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Email (from our @italki email)</h3>
<p>We contact teachers from our own work emails about:</p>
<ul>
<li>New projects where we need to approach the teacher as a consultant, freelancer, volunteer, etc. (Especially if it's just one or a small number of teachers.)</li>
<li>The person who needs the information should be the one to send the email, since often teachers forget to "reply all".</li>
<li>Teacher Services can help write the email to make sure it is framed in a way that will get a positive and cooperative response from teachers (they often don't understand "marketing" type messages very easily)</li>
<li>Teachers usually like us and are excited to work with us. This helps them feel like they are part of italki, just from outside of the office :) </li>
</ul>
<h3>Mass Messages</h3>
<p>For announcements, updates, etc. where we need to send the same message to a lot of teachers.</p>
<h3>Internal Message</h3>
<p>Internal messages are used to communicate with teachers about:</p>
<ul>
<li>Admin announcements</li>
<li>Status updates to their profile</li>
<li>Anything that should be part of an "official record" of communications between italki and the teacher</li>
<li>Use <a href="https://omsv2.italki.com/omsc4/messagesender" target="_blank" rel="noopener">OMS Mass Message Sender</a>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Note that you can only send a couple thousand at once. If you send too many, then some people will just not get the message. We think that 2000-3000 seems to be ok to send at once.</p>

<h3>Email</h3>
<p>Email is used to communicate with teachers about:</p>
<ul>
<li>A copy of something we sent to their internal italki message inbox</li>
<li>Something that we need to get teachers' attention about even if they don't log in and check.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>From Zetahub &lt;-- use this</strong></p>
<p>This is helpful because we can see click-through rates, open rates, and other metrics to help us know if the teachers are interested in the emails.</p>
<p>1. Write and check draft</p>
<p>2. Prepare Boomtrain Template</p>
<p>3. Check and Schedule with Roberta</p>
<p>4. Check open and click rates later</p>
